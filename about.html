Why We Work on AI

At SFU MDAI Club, we believe that Artificial Intelligence is shaping the future of technology and society. We are passionate about AI because:

  • AI has the potential to solve complex problems and improve various industries.
  • It offers exciting career opportunities and fosters innovation.
  • We aim to be at the forefront of technological advancements.

How We Work on AI

We are committed to exploring AI through various avenues, including:

  • Organizing lectures and seminars to facilitate learning and discussions.
  • Engaging in reading and writing research papers to stay updated with AI developments.
  • Participating in coding and research projects to apply AI concepts.
  • Competing in AI competitions to challenge and enhance our skills.
  • Conducting AI product analysis to understand real-world applications.
  • Attending and organizing AI conferences to connect with experts and enthusiasts.

What We Do

The SFU MDAI Club is actively involved in the following AI-related activities:

  • Organizing lectures and seminars featuring AI experts.
  • Facilitating reading and writing groups for AI research papers.
  • Running coding and research projects on AI topics.
  • Participating in AI competitions and hackathons.
  • Conducting AI product analysis and reviews.
  • Attending AI conferences and workshops.
